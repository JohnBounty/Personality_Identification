Personality profile analysis

Given input was classified to cluster 0 or “Executive”. Its highest scores are conscientiousness, structure, curiosity and challenge. The values are not very well defined, the highest being self-enhancement and openness to change, approaching 0.5. Needs, such as Liberty, Ideal or Self-expression are very low. So, the ones belonging to the cluster are disciplined, diligent people aiming to succeed and understand what they are doing. However, they seem to ignore the abstract individual concepts such as a liberty, mostly functioning in the material part of the world.
